The constellations of your Starry Form improve. The <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d8">1d8</span> of the Archer and the Chalice becomes <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d8">2d8</span>, and while the Dragon is active, you have a Fly Speed of 20 feet and can hover.

Moreover, at the start of each of your turns while in your Starry Form, you can change which constellation glimmers on your body.
