The magic of your runes permanently alters you. When you gain this feature, roll <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="3d4">3d4</span>. You grow a number of inches in height equal to the roll.

Moreover, the extra damage you deal with your Giant's Might feature increases to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d8">1d8</span>.
