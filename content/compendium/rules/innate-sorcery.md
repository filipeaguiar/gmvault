---
title: "Innate Sorcery"
params:
  kind: "rule"
draft: true
status: "draft"
summary: "Habilidade de classe."
---

An event in your past left an indelible mark on you, infusing you with simmering magic. As a XPHB, you can unleash that magic for 1 minute, during which you gain the following benefits:

* The spell save DC of your Sorcerer spells increases by 1.

* You have XPHB on the attack rolls of Sorcerer spells you cast.

You can use this feature twice, and you regain all expended uses of it when you finish a XPHB.
