You can take a Bonus action to enter Rage if you aren’t wearing Heavy Armor. You can use Rage  times per Long Rest, and regain one expended use when you finish a Short Rest.

Activate Rage by clicking on this feature and selecting the drop down called Activate Rage. Deselect it to stop Raging.
