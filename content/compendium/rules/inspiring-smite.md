Immediately after you cast Divine Smite, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity and distribute Temporary Hit Points to creatures of your choice within 30 feet of yourself, which can include you. The total number of Temporary Hit Points equals <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d8">2d8</span> plus your Paladin level, divided among the chosen creatures however you like.
