---
title: "Circle Spell: Prolong"
params:
  kind: "rule"
draft: true
status: "draft"
summary: "Habilidade de classe."
---

When you cast a spell that has a duration of 1 min or longer, you can increase the duration of the spell depending on the number of secondary casters contributing to the spell.

Each secondary caster contributing to the spell must expend a spell slot (no action required). If the spell fails, these spell slots aren’t expended.
