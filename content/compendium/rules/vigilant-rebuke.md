15th-level Oath of the Watchers feature

You've learned how to chastise anyone who dares wield beguilements against you and your wards. Whenever you or a creature you can see within 30 feet of you succeeds on an Intelligence, a Wisdom, or a Charisma saving throw, you can use your reaction to deal <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d8">2d8</span> + your Charisma modifier force damage to the creature that forced the saving throw.
