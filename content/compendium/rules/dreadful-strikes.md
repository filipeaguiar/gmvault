You can augment your weapon strikes with mind-scarring magic drawn from the murky hollows of the Feywild. When you hit a creature with a weapon, you can deal an extra <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d4">1d4</span> Psychic damage to the target, which can take this extra damage only once per turn. The extra damage increases to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> when you reach Ranger level 11.
