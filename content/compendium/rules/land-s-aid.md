As a Magic action, you can expend a use of your Wild Shape and choose a point within 60 feet of yourself. Vitality-giving flowers and life-draining thorns appear for a moment in a 10-foot-radius Sphere centered on that point. Each creature of your choice in the Sphere must make a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC, taking <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d6">2d6</span> Necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. One creature of your choice in that area regains <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d6">2d6</span> Hit Points.

The damage and healing increase by <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> when you reach Druid levels 10 (<span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="3d6">3d6</span>) and 14 (<span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="4d6">4d6</span>).
