You can tap into your inner wellspring of magic to try to conjure success from failure. When you make an ability check that fails, you can spend 1 sorcery point to reroll the <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="d20">d20</span>, and you must use the new roll, potentially turning the failure into a success.
