Your spellcasting can unleash surges of untamed magic. Once per turn, you can roll <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d20">1d20</span> immediately after you cast a Sorcerer spell with a spell slot. If you roll a 20, roll on the Wild Magic Surge table to create a magical effect.

If the magical effect is a spell, it is too wild to be affected by your Metamagic.
