---
title: "Initiate a Circle Spell"
params:
  kind: "rule"
draft: true
status: "draft"
summary: "Habilidade de classe."
---

You take a Magic action to initiate casting a Circle spell. When you do so, choose which Circle casting option you’re using for this casting; you must also meet any of the other requirements described in that option’s text. Until the Circle spell’s casting is complete, you must maintain Concentration on the spell.
