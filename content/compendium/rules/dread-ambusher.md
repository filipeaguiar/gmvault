You have mastered the art of creating fearsome ambushes, granting you the following benefits.


### Ambusher's Leap

At the start of your first turn of each combat, your Speed increases by 10 feet until the end of that turn.


### Dreadful Strike

When you attack a creature and hit it with a weapon, you can deal an extra <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d6">2d6</span> Psychic damage. You can use this benefit only once per turn, you can use it a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.


### Initiative Bonus

When you roll Initiative, you can add your Wisdom modifier to the roll.
