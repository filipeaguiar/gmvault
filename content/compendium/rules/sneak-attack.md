You know how to strike subtly and exploit a foe's distraction. Once per turn, you can deal an extra <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> damage to one creature you hit with an attack roll if you have XPHB on the roll and the attack uses a Finesse or a Ranged weapon. The extra damage's type is the same as the weapon's type.

You don't need XPHB on the attack roll if at least one of your allies is within 5 feet of the target, the ally doesn't have the XPHB condition, and you don't have XPHB on the attack roll.

The extra damage increases as you gain Rogue levels, as shown in the Sneak Attack column of the Rogue Features table.
