18th-level Rune Knight feature

You learn how to amplify your rune-powered transformation. As a result, the extra damage you deal with the Giant's Might feature increases to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d10">1d10</span>. Moreover, when you use that feature, your size can increase to Huge, and while you are that size, your reach increases by 5 feet.
