You add a toxin to your strike, forcing the target to make a DC {{savedc:dex}} Con. saving throw. On a failed save, the target has the Poisoned condition for 1 minute, and at the end of each of its turns, the Poisoned target repeats the save, ending the effect on itself on a success. You must have a Poisoner’s Kit on your person to use this effect.
