If you use Reckless Attack, you can forgo any XPHB on one Strength-based attack roll of your choice on your turn. The chosen attack roll mustn't have XPHB. If the chosen attack roll hits, the target takes an extra 1d10 damage of the same type dealt by the weapon or XPHB, and you can cause one Brutal Strike effect of your choice.
