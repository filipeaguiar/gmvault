<strong><em>Cleave.</em></strong> Once per turn, if you hit a creature with a melee attack using a Greataxe, you can make another melee attack with it against a second creature within 5 ft. of the first that’s within your reach. On a hit, the second creature takes the Greataxe’s damage, but without your ability modifier (unless the modifier is negative).
