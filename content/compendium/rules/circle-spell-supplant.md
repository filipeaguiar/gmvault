---
title: "Circle Spell: Supplant"
params:
  kind: "rule"
draft: true
status: "draft"
summary: "Habilidade de classe."
---

When you cast a spell that requires at least one Material component with a specified cost that is consumed by the spell, you can reduce the min cost of one such Material component by 50 GP per each secondary caster contributing to the spell.

Each secondary caster contributing to the spell must expend a spell slot of a level greater than or equal to the spell’s level (no action required). If the spell fails, these spell slots aren’t expended.
