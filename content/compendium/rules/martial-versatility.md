Whenever you reach a level in this class that grants the Ability Score Improvement feature, you can do one of the following, as you shift the focus of your martial practice:

* Replace a feature type=FS:F you know with another fighting style available to fighters.

* If you know any feature type=MV:B from the 2-0 archetype, you can replace one maneuver you know with a different maneuver.
