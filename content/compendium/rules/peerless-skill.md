When you make an ability check or attack roll and fail, you can expend one use of Bardic Inspiration; roll the Bardic Inspiration die, and add the number rolled to the <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="d20">d20</span>, potentially turning a failure into a success. On a failure, the Bardic Inspiration isn't expended.
