At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with the fiery power of the forge. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d8">1d8</span> fire damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d8">2d8</span>.
