Starting at 15th level, you can run down your foes, whether you're mounted or not. If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line right before attacking a creature and you hit it with the attack, that target must succeed on a Strength saving throw (8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier) or be knocked prone. You can use this feature only once on each of your turns.
