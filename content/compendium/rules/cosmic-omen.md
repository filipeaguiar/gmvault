Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can consult your Star Map for omens and roll a die. Until you finish your next Long Rest, you gain access to a special Reaction based on whether you rolled an even or an odd number on the die:

* {'type': 'item', 'name': 'Weal (even)', 'entries': ['Whenever a creature you can see within 30 feet of you is about to make a D20 Test, you can take a Reaction to roll <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> and add the number rolled to the total.']}

* {'type': 'item', 'name': 'Woe (odd)', 'entries': ['Whenever a creature you can see within 30 feet of you is about to make a D20 Test, you can take a Reaction to roll <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> and subtract the number rolled from the total.']}

You can use this Reaction a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
