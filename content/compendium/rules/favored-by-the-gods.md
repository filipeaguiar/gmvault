Starting at 1st level, divine power guards your destiny. If you fail a saving throw or miss with an attack roll, you can roll <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d4">2d4</span> and add it to the total, possibly changing the outcome. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
