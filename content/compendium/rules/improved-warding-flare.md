You regain all expended uses of your Warding Flare when you finish a Short or Long Rest.

In addition, whenever you use Warding Flare, you can give the target of the triggering attack a number of Temporary Hit Points equal to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d6">2d6</span> plus your Wisdom modifier.
