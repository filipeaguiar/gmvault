title: "Circle Spell: Augment"
params:
  kind: "rule"
draft: false
status: "ready"
summary: "Habilidade de classe."
---

When you cast a spell with a range of at least 5 ft., you can increase the range of the spell by 1,000 ft. per secondary caster contributing to the spell, up to a max of a 1-mile increase.
