You can use a bonus action on your turn to make your ranged attacks with a kensei weapon more deadly. When you do so, any target you hit with a ranged attack using a kensei weapon takes an extra <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d4">1d4</span> damage of the weapon's type. You retain this benefit until the end of the current turn.
