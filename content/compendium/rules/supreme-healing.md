When you would normally roll one or more dice to restore Hit Points to a creature with a spell or Channel Divinity, don't roll those dice for the healing; instead use the highest number possible for each die. For example, instead of restoring <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="2d6">2d6</span> Hit Points to a creature with a spell, you restore 12.
