Starting at 15th level, a holy presence mends your wounds in battle. You regain hit points equal to <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> + half your paladin level if you end your turn in combat with fewer than half of your hit points remaining and you aren't incapacitated.
