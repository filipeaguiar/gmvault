At 7th level, you gain extra resilience against your prey's assaults on your mind and body. Whenever the target of your Slayer's Prey forces you to make a saving throw and whenever you make an ability check to escape that target's grapple, add <span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d6">1d6</span> to your roll.
