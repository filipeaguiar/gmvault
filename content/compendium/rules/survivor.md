You attain the pinnacle of resilience in battle, giving you these benefits.


### Defy Death

You have XPHB on Death Saving Throws. Moreover, when you roll 18–20 on a XPHB, you gain the benefit of rolling a 20 on it.


### Heroic Rally

At the start of each of your turns, you regain XPHB equal to 5 plus your Constitution modifier if you are XPHB and have at least 1 Hit Point.
