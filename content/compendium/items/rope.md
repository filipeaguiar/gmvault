As a XPHB action, you can tie a knot with Rope if you succeed on a 10 Dexterity (XPHB) check. The Rope can be burst with a successful 20 Strength (XPHB) check.

You can bind an unwilling creature with the Rope only if the creature has the XPHB, XPHB, or XPHB condition. If the creature's legs are bound, the creature has the XPHB condition until it escapes. Escaping the Rope requires the creature to make a successful 15 Dexterity (XPHB) check as an action.
