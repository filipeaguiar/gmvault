## Traits


### Consume Life

As a bonus action, the will-o'-wisp can target one creature it can see within 5 feet of it that has 0 hit points and is still alive. The target must succeed on a 10 Constitution saving throw against this magic or die. If the target dies, the will-o'-wisp regains 10 (<span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="3d6">3d6</span>) hit points.


### Ephemeral

The will-o'-wisp can't wear or carry anything.


### Incorporeal Movement

The will-o'-wisp can move through other creatures and objects as if they were 3. It takes 5 (<span class="dice+" data-roll-notation="1d10">1d10</span>) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.


### Variable Illumination

The will-o'-wisp sheds bright light in a 5 to 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional number of ft. equal to the chosen radius. The will-o'-wisp can alter the radius as a bonus action.
