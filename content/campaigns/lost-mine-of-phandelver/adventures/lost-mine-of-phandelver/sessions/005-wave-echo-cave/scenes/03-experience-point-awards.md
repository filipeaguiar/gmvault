### Descrição e Elementos Importantes

In this part of the adventure, XP is awarded for overcoming monsters, as in parts 2 and 3. However, XP awards for monsters are not given in the encounter description. Instead, the amount of XP that a monster is worth is noted in its stat block. You calculate the award by totaling the value for each monster the characters overcome. Additional XP awards, and the reasons for them, are described in the text, under the "Awarding Experience Points" heading.
