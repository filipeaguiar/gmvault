### Descrição e Elementos Importantes

*An Adventure for 10th-Level Characters*

When a legendary dragon known as a bakunawa appears and attacks a town that has long revered its kind, the characters are called upon to learn why it has gone on a rampage. They must track down the bakunawa and discover the reason for its ire before dragon hunters slay the sacred creature.
